About Me


My name is Tom Polkinghorne and I am currently in year 12 at Scotch College. For my year 12 VCE studies, I am doing English, Further Maths, Business Management, Software Development and Informatics. In Unit 4, Area of study 1 for Informatics, I was required to produce a Multimodal online solution that displays information on data observed that relates to a specific hypothesis that needed to be investigated. Throughout this SAT, I investigated whether the inclusion of the AFLW has caused more young girls to further pursue Australian Rules Football at any level.

This hypothesis that I studied is something that I have vested interest in. By observing the growth of females playing Aussie rules was something that really interested me, as I wanted to see how much of an impact that the AFLW has on my hypothesis.

My hypothesis was an area of interest due to the extensive sport that I participate in. For Scotch College in my VCE years, I have played 1st XI Cricket, 1st XVIII Football, 1sts Basketball, 2nds Volleyball and vice-captain of Athletics. Due to me being keen and passionate on my sport, I found it enjoyable to gather data from sources and myself on a topic that is related to sport!

Hopefully you enjoy my site!
